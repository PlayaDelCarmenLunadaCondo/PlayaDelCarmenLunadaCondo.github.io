Lunada Condo In Playa Del Carmen, MX

Enjoy this 2 bedroom, 2.5 bathroom spacious condo with seating in the terrace overlooking a lush garden in the courtyard. Pour yourself a drink or cup of coffee, kick off your shoes and enjoy the tranquility Lunada has to offer. Ready for a dip in the pool or the jacuzzi, take the elevator or the stairs to the rooftop and enjoy the sunset. Throw some steaks on the bbq and relax. You can also hit the gym down stairs if you're in the mood for a workout. Famous 5th Ave at a 5 min walk, and beach.

Info

Want to relax? Come to Playa Del Carmen. A beautiful condo with a pool and 7 minutes from the beach and one block away from the famous 5th avenue. Lunada is located on Calle 28 and Avenida 20. It is in a great location surrounded by many great restaurants and stores. There are also a lot of family friendly places and alot of waterparks.

Things to Know about the place

This condo is equipped with 2 bedrooms including a sofa bed, 3 baths, balcony, a stovetop and a tv. It also has air conditioning and is pet friendly.
